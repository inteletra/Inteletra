Frequently Asked Questions - AI Property Platform & Legal Assistant

How does your AI property listing platform work?

Our AI property platform analyzes thousands of property listings in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah to match buyers with perfect properties at optimal prices. The AI considers your preferences, budget, location needs, and market trends to show you the best options while helping sellers reach qualified buyers.

What can your AI legal assistant help me with?

Our AI legal assistant provides instant guidance on UAE law matters including property transactions, contract queries, legal documentation questions, and general legal advice. It's available 24/7 as a self-service platform to get quick answers to your legal questions.

How accurate is the AI property price analysis?

Our AI algorithms analyze comprehensive market data, comparable sales, location factors, and real-time market trends across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah to provide highly accurate price analysis and recommendations for both buyers and sellers.

Is the AI legal assistant a replacement for real lawyers?

No, our AI legal assistant is designed to provide initial guidance and help with basic legal questions. For complex legal matters, formal documentation, or court proceedings, we always recommend consulting with qualified legal professionals.
